Tatsuki Fujimoto

Tatsuki Fujimoto is a Japanese manga artist, who has won multiple awards and has mutiple succesful series on his belt.

On the top is the diifferent series the artist has done.

Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man is the artist popular series he has written and is currently writing and drawing it's continuation.

This series is about a devil hunter named Denji who can transform his body into chainsaws and must defeat the Gun devil to fulfill his goal with a girl.

Look Back

Look Back is a one shot manga created by Fujimoto after doing the first part of Chainsaw Man.

Look Back is about two middle schoolers named Fujino and Kyomoto who were rivals in elementary then became friends, and journyed to become manga artist. Then a trajic incident hits for the both of them.
